OUR PURPOSE

Mission & Vision

Our Mission
  • To automate and simplify examination and timetable management processes.
  • To ensure efficient allocation of rooms, faculty duties, and academic resources.
  • To enhance accuracy, transparency, and productivity in institutional administration.
Our Vision

To transform examination and academic scheduling management through intelligent automation, seamless coordination, and efficient resource utilization — empowering educational institutions with a smarter and paperless administrative ecosystem.

THE CHALLENGE

Why Your Institution Needs This

Educational institutions still rely on fragmented, error-prone manual systems — creating daily headaches for staff and management.

Scattered Excel Sheets

No single source of truth — timetables, duties & records all over the place, leading to version chaos.

Human Error & Bias

Unfair duty distribution, overlapping assignments & leave conflicts that damage staff morale.

Wasted Admin Hours

Days spent manually scheduling what should take hours — every single semester, repeatedly.

Disconnected Departments

No visibility across departments — everyone works in silos with no coordination or transparency.

↓Timetable Pro solves all of this
WHAT IT DOES

Powerful Features, One Platform

Everything your institution needs to manage academic scheduling and examination duties — beautifully unified.

Smart Timetable Management

Create, clone & publish conflict-free timetables with automated room & faculty allocation. Smart conflict detection catches clashes before they happen.

  • One-click timetable cloning for fast semester setup
  • Faculty-wise, department-wise, semester-wise & day-wise schedule views
  • Intelligent room allocation with conflict detection
  • Batch-wise & lab-wise scheduling support
  • Public & internal timetable publishing with role-based access control

Automated Exam Duty Generation

Rule-based fair allocation — balances workload, respects leaves, eliminates bias. One click generates duties for the entire institution.

  • Automated invigilation duty generation with intelligent workload balancing
  • Advanced duty allocation considering leaves, previous duties & department distribution
  • Reserve & relief invigilator management for emergency replacements
  • Multi-stage approval workflow for secure examination processing
  • Real-time notifications & email alerts for duty assignments

FYUG Curriculum Support

Handles 663+ major & minor combinations with full academic data management and bulk Excel import.

Announcements & Leave Tracking

Integrated notifications, leave requests, approval workflows & faculty leave management integrated directly with invigilation scheduling.

Audit Logs & Data Security

Digital examination records and audit tracking supporting NAAC documentation. Secure workflow with audit logs, backup & restore, and multi-user management.

Room & Department Management

Centralized subject, department & room management. One-click room assignment with automatic room conflict prevention.

Duty Certificate Generation

Auto-generate and download invigilation duty certificates for faculty on demand. Centralized examination dashboard with session-wise analytics.

Bulk Excel Import

Bulk import for subjects, rooms, departments, timetable data and allocations. Streamlined academic data onboarding for fast setup.
